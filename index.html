About Us

Discover the true taste of Italy with our authentic Italian pizza, crafted with passion and tradition. Each pizza begins with hand-kneaded dough made from premium Italian flour, left to rise naturally for a light, airy crust. It’s then topped with rich San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella di bufala, extra virgin olive oil, and a selection of gourmet ingredients – from aromatic basil and sun-ripened vegetables to cured meats and truffles. Baked to perfection in a traditional wood-fired oven, our pizzas offer a crisp, slightly charred base and a tender center – just like in Naples. Whether you choose a classic Margherita, a bold Diavola, or a luxurious Prosciutto e Funghi, every slice is a journey to the heart of Italy.

Creamy Pasta

₹180

Creamy pasta is a rich and flavorful dish made by combining cooked pasta with a smooth, velvety sauce, usually made from cream, butter, and cheese. The sauce coats the pasta perfectly, creating a comforting and indulgent meal. Common additions like garlic, onions, and herbs such as parsley or basil enhance the flavor, while ingredients like chicken, mushrooms, or shrimp can be added for variety. Popular versions include fettuccine Alfredo and creamy tomato or mushroom pasta. Loved for its simplicity and satisfying taste, creamy pasta is a go-to comfort food for many.
